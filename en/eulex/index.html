How EuleX Works

1

Submit your query

Enter your search term on the EuleX website. Your request is forwarded directly to the Google Custom Search API without being cached.

2

Receive results

EuleX processes the raw data from Google and removes all tracking elements and advertisements before displaying the results.

3

Browse safely

You can open search results without EuleX tracking your click behavior or storing personal data.
